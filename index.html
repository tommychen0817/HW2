Mission

myLife is a non-profit foundation dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for individuals and communities by addressing physical, emotional, and social well-being. Our mission is to provide holistic support, resources, and initiatives that empower people to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Our Projects

Community Wellness Programs

myLife designs and implements wellness programs in local communities, focusing on physical fitness, mental health, nutrition, and social engagement. These programs aim to improve overall well-being and foster a sense of belonging.

Youth Empowerment Programs

The foundation runs programs that empower young people through education, mentorship, and skill development, helping them make positive life choices and achieve their potential.

Community Gardens and Food Initiatives

myLife promotes sustainable food sources through community gardens, food drives, and partnerships with local farmers. Access to fresh, nutritious food is seen as vital to overall quality of life.
